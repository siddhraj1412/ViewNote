
UI/UX Design Document
Project: Movie &amp; Series Tracking Platform
Theme: Cinematic &amp; Moody (Dark Mode First)
Inspiration: Letterboxd Dribbble Redesigns
1. Design Philosophy
The application follows a cinematic, immersive dark-theme design.Focus is on minimal distractions, strong visuals, and calm user experience.Posters and banners are primary visual elements.
